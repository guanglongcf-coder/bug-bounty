# The Freelancer's Code

## Stanza I: The Marketplace

In digital halls where contracts meet the byte,
Where API calls weave futures in the night,
A marketplace of talent finds its home—
No borders hold, and no one works alone.
From Prisma schemas, job posts come alive,
And through the wire, freelancers thrive.

## Stanza II: The Transaction

When handshake's sealed in cryptographic trust,
And milestones crumble into settled dust,
The escrow smart contract begins to sing—
A proven payload taking wing.
From commit hash to deployment's grace,
Code becomes commerce in this cyber-space.

## Stanza III: The Dream

Beyond the curl and the JSON reply,
Behind the endpoints stacked against the sky,
Lies what we built when deadlines had their way—
A market for the work we do each day.
So let the tests pass green and servers run,
For every coder chasing down the sun.

---

*Written for the FreelanceFlow project — a poem about the marketplace that connects those who build with those who need.*
